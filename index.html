About

The Aternos GMBH Wiki is an unofficial source created to support people in the making, maintaining and securing of their servers with articles that are not explained in the official support center. It also displays articles that explain various topics about Aternos/Minecraft which have often been mistaken, weren't clear or aren't explained.
